8-Week Course Structure

Week 1: Gmail

Master professional email management, organization, and communication strategies for efficient correspondence.

Week 2: Google Docs + Google Drive

Learn professional document creation, collaborative editing, and cloud file management with advanced sharing permissions.

Week 3: Google Calendar + Google Meet

Master scheduling, event management, and virtual meeting coordination for optimal productivity and communication.

Week 4: Google Forms

Create powerful online surveys, questionnaires, and data collection forms with advanced features and analytics.

Week 5: Google Sheets

Create powerful spreadsheets, data analysis, formulas, charts, and automated reporting for business insights.

Week 6: Google Keep

Organize thoughts with digital note-taking, to-do lists, reminders, and idea management systems.

Week 7: Trello

Implement project management workflows, team collaboration boards, and task tracking systems for productivity.

workspace_premium

Week 8: Graduation/Closing

Final assessment and certification completion for Administrative Toolkit mastery.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the training? expand_more

8 weeks total: 7 weeks covering essential productivity tools (Gmail, Docs+Drive, Calendar+Meet, Forms, Sheets, Keep, Trello) plus a final graduation/certification week.

What if I miss a class? expand_more

All course materials remain accessible throughout the program. You can review previous weeks' content at any time. Consistent absences may lead to removal from the program to maintain group progress.

Who is eligible? expand_more

Open to anyone wanting to build administrative skills. No prior experience needed, just willingness to learn and commit.
